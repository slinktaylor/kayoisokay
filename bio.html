about kayo

creating music that feels like floating through cotton candy clouds, okaykayo transforms everyday emotions into dreamy soundscapes that wrap around your heart like a warm hug.

born from late-night journal entries and sunrise drives, each song carries the weight of whispered secrets and the lightness of butterfly wings. kayo believes music should be a safe space where vulnerability meets beauty, where broken hearts learn to dance again.

influenced by the colors of sunset skies and the rhythm of ocean waves, the sound blends indie pop melodies with ethereal production, creating an atmosphere where listeners can lose themselves and find themselves all at once.
